Objective of dream project

tion technologies in the 21st-century. 5G is the next step beyond 4G and LTE mobile net- works. It has faster speeds, high bandwidth and a wider range than the previous networks. Educators have been always looking for ways to maximise the learning time of students. Technology helps teachers to make better connections with students and explore compli- cated topics. But buffering videos and choppy applications can diminish the precious time available, limiting that time educators have to actually teach. When it comes to the 5G, there are already plenty of applications in manufacturing, media, city management, and healthcare. Education is just scratching the surface of what’s possible in the classroom. With the arrival of 5G, the teachers and educa- tors can be able to expect a lot of new teaching experience and the student can be able to enjoy the new learning experience. Augmented real- ity (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR) which includes mixed-reality content and video can help the students to give more immersive lessons. It helps the students to navigate through the human body or visit other planets.
